Healthcare & Medical

Hospitals, Clinics, Medical Practices

Healthcare organizations operate under unique constraints. Patient data privacy is non-negotiable. System downtime directly impacts care delivery. Clinical staff have limited time to learn new tools. And every digital touchpoint from appointment booking to test results affects patient experience and trust.

Understanding Healthcare Operations

We've worked with healthcare providers ranging from multi-specialty clinics to specialized treatment centers. This experience has given us deep understanding of clinical workflows, patient communication patterns, and integration requirements with existing HIS (Hospital Information Systems) and EMR (Electronic Medical Records).

What We Build for Healthcare

Patient-Facing Applications

Mobile and web applications that allow patients to book appointments, access medical records, communicate with providers, receive medication reminders, and manage their health journey.

  • • Online appointment scheduling with provider availability
  • • Secure medical record access
  • • Prescription refill requests
  • • Telehealth video consultations
  • • Push notifications for appointments and reminders

Clinical Staff Platforms

Web-based management systems for clinical and administrative staff, designed to minimize data entry, quickly surface relevant information, and support efficient patient care.

  • • Patient management dashboards
  • • Appointment management and optimization
  • • Clinical documentation tools
  • • Internal communication systems
  • • Reporting and analytics

Administrative Systems

Back-office tools for practice management, including financial reporting, resource allocation, and operational visibility across departments.

  • • Financial and billing dashboards
  • • Resource utilization tracking
  • • Staff scheduling and management
  • • Compliance and audit reporting

Security and Compliance

Every healthcare system we build is designed with security as a fundamental concern, not an afterthought. This includes end-to-end encryption, role-based access control, comprehensive audit logging, and compliance with KVKK (Turkish Data Protection Law) and GDPR requirements where applicable.

E-commerce & Retail

Online Retailers, Multi-Channel Operations

E-commerce operations face a fundamental scaling problem: as order volumes grow, manual processes that worked at low volumes become bottlenecks. Inventory accuracy drops, fulfillment times lengthen, and marketplace integrations become fragile. The solution is systematic automation of operational workflows.

The Multi-Channel Challenge

Today, most e-commerce businesses sell through multiple channels: their own websites, marketplaces like Trendyol and Hepsiburada, and potentially Amazon or international platforms. Each channel has its own order format, inventory requirements, and fulfillment expectations. Without centralized systems, teams manage each channel separately, duplicating effort and introducing errors.

What We Build for E-commerce

Order Management Systems

Centralized order processing that aggregates orders from all channels, applies business rules for routing and prioritization, and tracks fulfillment status through delivery.

  • • Multi-channel order aggregation
  • • Automated order routing and prioritization
  • • Fulfillment workflow management
  • • Shipping integration and tracking
  • • Returns and exchanges processing

Inventory Management

Real-time inventory tracking across warehouses and sales channels, with automatic synchronization to prevent overselling and stockouts.

  • • Real-time stock level tracking
  • • Multi-warehouse management
  • • Automatic channel synchronization
  • • Low stock alerts and reorder triggers
  • • Inventory forecasting

Marketplace Integrations

API-based integrations with major Turkish and international marketplaces, managing product listing synchronization, order import, and status updates.

  • • Trendyol, Hepsiburada, N11 integrations
  • • Amazon Seller Central integration
  • • Product catalog synchronization
  • • Price and availability updates
  • • Order and fulfillment synchronization

Operational Dashboards

Real-time visibility into key operational metrics, enabling proactive management of fulfillment performance, inventory health, and channel economics.

  • • Order and fulfillment KPIs
  • • Channel performance comparison
  • • Inventory health metrics
  • • Customer service analytics

Scalable Infrastructure

E-commerce has seasonal peaks—holiday periods, discount events, marketing campaigns. We design systems with infrastructure that handles traffic spikes seamlessly, scales automatically, and degrades gracefully under unexpected load.

SME & Enterprise

Growing Businesses, Digital Transformation

Small and medium enterprises are the backbone of Turkey's economy, but many operate with fragmented digital tools—or no tools at all. Spreadsheets serve as databases. Email chains replace proper workflows. And critical business knowledge lives in individual employees' heads rather than in institutional systems.

The Transformation Opportunity

Digital transformation for SMEs isn't about adopting enterprise software designed for much larger organizations. It's about building systems that match how your business actually operates, then systematically improving those operations. The goal is operational visibility, process reliability, and a foundation for continued growth.

What We Build for SMEs

Custom Management Systems

Purpose-built systems that digitize your core business processes. Unlike off-the-shelf software that forces you to adapt to its workflows, we build systems that match how you work—then optimize from there.

  • • Customer relationship management (CRM)
  • • Project and task management
  • • Service delivery tracking
  • • Quote and proposal management
  • • Vendor coordination

Process Digitization

Converting manual, paper-based, or email-driven processes into structured digital workflows with clear steps, accountability, and audit trails.

  • • Approval and authorization workflows
  • • Document management and version control
  • • Request and ticketing systems
  • • Compliance and checklist management
  • • Automated notifications and escalations

Reporting & Analytics

Dashboards and reports that give management real-time visibility into business performance, replacing the monthly scramble to compile spreadsheets.

  • • Real-time KPI dashboards
  • • Automated report generation
  • • Trend analysis and forecasting
  • • Department and team performance
  • • Financial and operational metrics

System Integration

Connecting your custom systems with existing tools—accounting software, banking systems, government portals—eliminating duplicate data entry and ensuring consistency across systems.

  • • Accounting software integration (Logo, Mikro, etc.)
  • • Banking and payment system connections
  • • E-invoice and e-archive integration
  • • HR and payroll system connections

Our Approach to SME Projects

We start by understanding your current operations—not just what you want to build, but why you need it and what problems you're actually solving. This discovery process often reveals that the real need is different from the initial request, and addressing the root cause delivers more value than the originally envisioned solution.
