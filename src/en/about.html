About Bayes Digital

We Build Systems That Actually Work

We are a digital systems company that designs and implements integrated infrastructure for organizations that need more than off-the-shelf solutions. Our work spans healthcare, commerce, and enterprise operations—always focused on reliability, sustainability, and measurable outcomes.

Our Story

Bayes Digital was founded in 2019 by a team of engineers who had spent years watching organizations struggle with fragmented digital tools. We saw healthcare providers using five different systems that couldn't communicate with each other. We saw e-commerce operations running on spreadsheets and manual processes. We saw growing businesses with no visibility into their own operations.

The problem wasn't a lack of software—there was plenty of that. The problem was a lack of integrated systems designed around how organizations actually work. Off-the-shelf solutions were forcing businesses to adapt to software limitations, when it should be the other way around.

We founded Bayes Digital to take a different approach: understand operational reality first, then design and build systems that address real needs. This means spending more time on discovery, being willing to say "you don't need what you're asking for—you need this instead," and taking ownership of outcomes, not just deliverables.

Today, we're a team of 22 engineers, designers, and project managers based in Istanbul. We've delivered over 45 major system implementations across healthcare, commerce, and enterprise sectors. Our clients stay with us because the systems we build actually work—and we continue taking responsibility for their ongoing operation.

Why "Bayes"?

We take our name from Bayes' theorem—the mathematical framework for updating beliefs based on new evidence. This reflects our approach to building systems: start with hypotheses about what will work, test against reality, and continuously improve. Good systems evolve based on evidence, not assumptions.

Principles That Guide Our Work

These aren't idealistic statements—they are real principles that shape how we make decisions and evaluate our own performance.

Outcomes Over Outputs

We measure success by whether the system solves the problem, not by whether we delivered the feature list. A project that delivers everything requested but doesn't improve operations is a failure.

Maintainable by Design

Every system we build should be understandable and maintainable by any competent engineering team. We write clean code, document extensively, and avoid proprietary dependencies that would lock you to us.

Honest Assessment

We'll tell you if an idea won't work, if you're solving the wrong problem, or if you don't actually need what you're asking for. Short-term revenue isn't worth building something that will fail.

Security First

Security isn't a feature we add later—it's part of the architecture from day one. Especially in healthcare and finance contexts, this non-negotiable approach has protected our clients from breaches.

Collaborative Process

We work with your team, not in isolation. The people who will use the system are involved throughout development. This produces better outcomes and ensures adoption at launch.

Long-Term Ownership

We don't disappear after launch. We have ongoing support relationships with most of our clients because systems require maintenance, updates, and evolution. We continue taking responsibility for what we build.

How We Work

Our approach is designed to minimize risk and maximize the likelihood that the system we build actually solves your problem.

1

Discovery & Analysis

We start by understanding your current operations, not just your requirements list. This often reveals that the real problem is different from the initial request. We interview stakeholders, observe workflows, and analyze existing data to build a complete picture.

Typical duration: 2-4 weeks depending on complexity

2

Solution Design

Based on discovery findings, we design system architecture, user flows, and integration points. This phase produces detailed specifications you can review and approve before any code is written. We also identify risks and dependencies that may affect the project.

Deliverables: Technical specification, architecture diagram, project plan

3

Iterative Development

We work in two-week sprints, delivering working software at the end of each cycle. This allows you to see progress, provide feedback, and adjust priorities based on what you learn. We use automated testing to ensure quality and prevent regressions.

Cadence: Demo every 2 weeks, daily progress updates

4

Testing & Deployment

Before launch, we conduct comprehensive testing including user acceptance testing with your team. Deployment is carefully planned to minimize disruption, often using phased rollouts that start with a subset of users before expanding.

Includes: User training, documentation, go-live support

5

Ongoing Support & Evolution

Systems require ongoing attention—bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and adaptation to changing requirements. We offer support packages that keep your system healthy and evolving. Most of our clients maintain ongoing relationships.

Options: Monthly retainer, per-incident support, or fully managed service
